Welcome to my deli shop!

Modrowsky's Meats and Sandwiches is a local deli shop located in College Station, Texas, and owned by Cornell student and sandwich enthusiast Andrew Modrowsky, who operates this business as a side job to his studies as an Information Science and Government double major. We provide a number of meats and sandwiches which you can order in person at our store, online through our menu, or in bulk through catering. We appreciate your business and interest in our sandwiches and meats, and welcome sandwich lovers from all over to enjoy!
